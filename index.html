Suryakumar Yadav

Suryakumar Yadav, often referred to as "SKY," is an Indian cricketer known for his explosive batting style and innovative shot-making Suryakumar Yadav was born on September 14, 1990, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He comes from a humble family, with his father, Ashok Kumar Yadav, working as an engineer.

Records

2018 Breakout Season:

In the 2018 IPL, after being promoted to the top order, Suryakumar scored 512 runs, his first 500+ season, showcasing his adaptability to various batting positions. His highest IPL score, 82* not out, came in a critical match, displaying his ability to anchor the innings and accelerate in the final overs.
